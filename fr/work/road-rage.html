You're a bully in traffic. Me too. But only sometimes.

Language
(EN)
Format
Editorial feature / essay

L'agressivité au volant, le narcissisme et l'erreur fondamentale d'attribution. Pourquoi nous nous perdons au volant — et pourquoi les conducteurs de cabriolets klaxonnent moins.

Extrait du texte

"Sit a human behind a steering wheel and get him a set of wheels: the output is an uncontrollable projectile."

"We forget about good behaviour, because we feel invisible and untouchable in this armour, that is our car."

"We are actually all losers who think they are winners."
